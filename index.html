Black Ice Accidents: What Auto Insurance Really Covers

Learn what your auto policy actually covers when black ice sends your vehicle sliding.

March 26, 2026

Black ice is one of the most dangerous driving hazards in the Lowcountry during winter months. Invisible ice on bridges and overpasses can send your vehicle into a guardrail, ditch, or another car before you even realize what happened.

What your auto policy covers: Collision coverage pays for damage to your own vehicle, minus your deductible. Liability covers damages to others. Comprehensive does NOT apply — black ice is classified as a collision event.

Uninsured motorist coverage is critical when the OTHER driver on black ice hits you and has no insurance.

Swamp Fox recommends: Review your collision deductible, confirm UM/UIM coverage, and consider gap coverage. Call us at (843) 761-3999 for a free policy review.

Common Auto Insurance Mistakes That Cost Drivers Money

Minor insurance errors can quietly drain your wallet. Learn how to avoid the most common ones.

January 13, 2026

Common mistakes can leave you underinsured or overpaying. Here are the most expensive errors we see at Swamp Fox Agency.

1. Minimum liability only. SC requires just 25/50/25. We recommend at least 100/300/100.

2. Skipping uninsured motorist coverage. About 1 in 8 SC drivers are uninsured.

3. Not reviewing annually. Life changes — new car, new driver, paid-off loan — each could save or cost you hundreds.

Understanding Uninsured & Underinsured Motorist Coverage

What happens when the other driver doesn't have enough insurance? Here's how to protect yourself.

October 21, 2025

UM/UIM coverage is one of the most important — and most overlooked — parts of your auto policy. In SC, roughly 12% of drivers carry no insurance at all.

Uninsured Motorist (UM) covers your medical bills, lost wages, and pain/suffering when the at-fault driver has zero insurance.

Underinsured Motorist (UIM) fills the gap when the at-fault driver's limits aren't enough to cover your damages.

Talk to Swamp Fox: We'll match your UM/UIM limits for maximum protection. Call (843) 761-3999.
